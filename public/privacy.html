Privacy Policy

Effective date: 6 April 2026  |  Last updated: 6 April 2026

Readera (“we”, “us”, “our”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, how we protect it, and the choices you have. By using Readera (website, reader, checkout, and related services), you agree to this policy.

1. Who we are

Readera operates a digital library and e-book storefront. The data controller for personal data processed through this service is Readera (contact details in Section 12). If you use payment partners (e.g. Razorpay) or hosting providers, they process data under their own policies as independent processors where applicable.

2. Scope

This policy applies to information we collect when you visit our site, create an account, browse or purchase books, use the reader, contact support, or interact with marketing or analytics tools we may enable. It does not govern third-party sites linked from Readera; please read their policies separately.

3. Information we collect

3.1 You provide directly

  • Account data: name, email address, password (stored hashed—we do not store plain-text passwords).
  • Profile & preferences: optional display name, reading interests where you choose to provide them.
  • Transactions: items purchased, order references, billing country or currency where required for tax or compliance (payment card data is handled by our payment processor, not stored on our servers).
  • Support & contact: message content, email thread, attachments you send to support.

3.2 Collected automatically

  • Device & usage: IP address, browser type, approximate location (derived from IP), pages viewed, time on site, referring URL, crash or error logs.
  • Cookies & similar technologies: session identifiers, cart and login state, theme preferences, analytics or performance cookies where enabled.

3.3 From others

We may receive fraud signals, payment confirmation, or email delivery status from payment, email, or security vendors.

4. Legal bases (where GDPR-style rules apply)

Depending on your region, we rely on: contract (providing the service you requested); legitimate interests (security, analytics, product improvement, fraud prevention—balanced against your rights); consent (optional marketing, non-essential cookies); and legal obligation (tax, lawful requests).

5. How we use information

  • Create and secure your account; authenticate you.
  • Process purchases, deliver digital content, and manage your library.
  • Save reading progress locally or on our servers where the feature exists.
  • Communicate transactional messages (receipts, password reset, policy updates).
  • Improve reliability, fix bugs, and understand aggregate usage patterns.
  • Detect abuse, fraud, and enforce our Terms of Use.
  • Comply with law and respond to valid legal process.

6. Sharing and processors

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only:

  • With service providers (hosting, database, email, payments, analytics) under strict confidentiality and data-processing terms.
  • When required by law or to protect rights, safety, and integrity of users or Readera.
  • In connection with a business transfer (merger, acquisition)—users will be notified as required.

International users: where data is transferred across borders, we use appropriate safeguards (e.g. standard contractual clauses) where legally required.

7. Retention

We keep data only as long as needed for the purposes above: account data while your account is active; transactional records as required for tax and accounting; logs for a limited period for security; support tickets according to operational needs. You may request deletion subject to legal retention obligations.

8. Security

We use industry-standard measures (encryption in transit, access controls, secure credential storage). No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure; we encourage strong passwords and device security.

9. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, export, restrict, or object to certain processing, and to withdraw consent where processing is consent-based. To exercise rights, contact us (Section 12). You may lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

10. Children

Readera is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

11. Cookies

Essential cookies are required for login, cart, and security. Where we use analytics or marketing cookies, we will seek consent where required. You can control cookies through browser settings; disabling some cookies may limit features.

12. Contact & DPO

For privacy questions or requests: privacy@readera.com (replace with your live inbox when ready) or via our Contact page. Postal inquiries may be sent to the business address listed on Contact.

13. Changes

We may update this policy to reflect product or legal changes. We will post the new date at the top and, where appropriate, notify you by email or on-site notice. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance unless applicable law requires explicit consent.
