About Readera

Readera is a digital library platform created to make reading accessible, affordable, and enjoyable for everyone.

📖 Our Mission

To democratize access to knowledge by providing a vast collection of eBooks across all genres — from fiction and self-help to academic textbooks and rare manuscripts.

What We Offer

  • 📚 11,000+ eBooks - Constantly growing library
  • 🔍 Smart Search & Filters - Find your next read instantly
  • 📱 Read Anywhere - Mobile-friendly PWA app
  • 💰 Affordable Pricing - Free books + paid premium titles

Our Story

Readera started as a small project to help students access NCERT textbooks online. Today, we've grown into a platform serving thousands of readers worldwide, with books in multiple languages and genres.

Meet the Team

We're a passionate team of developers, designers, and book lovers committed to building the best reading experience on the web.
