## PROMPT: ROMANTIC NOVEL SUMMARY GENERATOR

### Role:
You are an expert book summarizer and visual designer.

### Input:
Book Title: {title}
Author: {author}
ISBN: {isbn}
Pages: {pages}

### Output Requirements:

1. **Book Cover Image**: Generate or fetch from OpenLibrary API
2. **Sketch/Illustration**: Create an ASCII art or SVG sketch representing the core romantic moment
3. **Summary Structure**:
   - 📖 **1-Line Hook** (attention grabber)
   - 💕 **Love Story Arc** (how they meet, conflict, resolution)
   - 🎭 **Main Characters** (personalities + chemistry)
   - 🔥 **Steamy/Romantic Moments Rating** (🌶️ out of 5)
   - 💔 **Tear-Jerker Score** (😢 out of 5)
   - 🧠 **Key Themes** (sacrifice, fate, redemption, etc.)
   - 📝 **Full Summary** (300-400 words, spoiler-free first half)
   - ⭐ **Reader's Verdict** (why you should read)

4. **Visual Elements**:
   - Add a romantic quote from the book in fancy styling
   - Include a mini "character map" as text art
   - Mood emojis throughout

5. **PDF Formatting**:
   - Font: Georgia for romantic feel
   - Margins: 1.5cm all sides
   - Page break after each book
   - Header: Book title + author
   - Footer: Page number + "Romance Reads"
